Offence card

Displays details about offences and sentence information.

When to use it

  • When users need to review or manage multiple offences in one place
  • To present offence details in a clear and structured format

How it works

  • Visually separates offences to improve readability
  • Organises offence details in a structured, index card-style layout
  • Can include actions for individual offences, such as editing or updating the outcome
  • Colour indicates the outcome status of the offence:
  • Grey – non-custodial or not sentenced
  • Purple – custodial outcome (i.e. imprisonment), indicating the offence is ‘activated’

Research & rationale

  • Helps users scan and manage offence information efficiently
  • Ensures offence and sentence details are clearly structured and easy to reference
