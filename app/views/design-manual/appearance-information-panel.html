Appearance information panel

Displays key details about a court appearance within journeys.

When to use it

  • When users need a quick reference to court appearance details
  • To provide context when reviewing or updating case information

How it works

  • The panel includes:
  • Appearance information – always shown when available
  • Offences – only displayed when offences have been added
  • The panel does not appear on the Add Appearance Information task, as no details have been entered yet
  • The offences section is hidden on pages where offences are already listed, such as the Add Offences page

Research & rationale

  • Ensures key details are surfaced without requiring users to navigate elsewhere
  • Provides clarity and reduces errors in data entry or decision-making
