Components

The Remand and sentencing service uses components from existing government design systems, along with custom components designed to meet specific needs in the service.

Exising design systems

Remand and Sentencing is built primarily using components and patterns from the GOV.UK Design System and the Ministry of Justice Design System. Some design elements and styles are also drawn from Digital Prison Services.

Where components from these systems are used in specific scenarios, they are documented in this design manual.

Custom components

Custom components have been created to meet specific needs in Remand and sentencing where no existing component was suitable.

These components can be reused by other services facing similar scenarios.
